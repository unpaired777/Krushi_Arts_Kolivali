About Us

Krushi Arts Kolivali – Ganesh Kala Kendra is more than just an art studio; it's a spiritual and cultural hub where tradition, devotion, and creativity come together. Founded with a passion for art and rooted in our rich cultural heritage, we specialize in:

  • Handcrafted POP Ganesh idols with attention to detail and devotion
  • Building, flat, and warehouse coloring to bring life and vibrance to spaces
  • Eco-friendly sculpture practices that respect nature and tradition
  • Custom mural and art design for interiors and community spaces

Every project we undertake is done with love, care, and a deep respect for the art form. Whether you’re looking to bring home a beautiful Ganpati idol or revamp your space with stunning paintwork, Krushi Arts is here to serve.
